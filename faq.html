Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What courses does Cyber Hub offer?

Cyber Hub offers courses in cybersecurity fundamentals, ethical hacking, penetration testing, OSINT, malware analysis, and more.

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply click on the "Enroll" button in the navigation bar or visit the enrollment page to fill out the form and complete your payment.

Are the courses self-paced or instructor-led?

Our courses offer a mix of both self-paced learning and live instructor-led sessions to accommodate different learning preferences.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept local payments popular in Bangladesh such as bKash, Nagad, Rocket, and card payments through SSLCommerz.

Will I receive a certificate upon course completion?

Yes, all students who successfully complete a course will receive an official Cyber Hub certificate.
